INDIAN PREMIER LEAGUE

The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a Twenty20 cricket league founded by the BCCI in 2008, with its inaugural season in 2008 featuring eight teams. The most successful franchises are Mumbai Indians and Chennai Super Kings, each with five titles. Over the years, the league has expanded to 10 teams and is one of the world's richest sports leagues, known for its high commercial investment and global viewership.
